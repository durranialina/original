The Pros of Stomp

Stomp is a dance competition where most teams across the GTA complete an audition and dress rehearsal in order to compete in a Competition in May or June this year JJM Bollywood Fusion is competing in the Stomp Competition against other teams

Creative themes

Every year when there is STOMP there are creative themes Such as Tradition, Evolution. This year the theme chosen for all teams to coreograph a dance to is Evolution.

The Competition Overall

May 6th 2023 was the official competition date for STOMP 2023 JJM Bollywood Fusion competed and won with 2nd place in the beginner category against the Rhythm & Vibes Group, Next Generation, The All Stars, Dance Divas, and Weston Mount Dennis & Lawrence Hieghts Boys & Girls club.

The Catalogue

This year JJM Bollywood Fusion was included in one of the pages in Page 9 of the Catelogue for 2023.

Conclusion

Overall, STOMP is a very good competition to compete in with your team, it encourages teamwork and you get to make new friends and advance to different categories when you go on with STOMP. Therefore I would recommend STOMP to others.
